About Us

The MotionEase Co. was established with body inclusivity as one of its advocacies. When looking for gaps in the market, the founders discovered a particular problem that members of the plus-size community frequently face, yet is not properly addressed—skin chafing.

This small and seemingly trivial problem may lead to greater concerns, and even interrupt our daily routine. As a solution for this, the Cozy Glide was created.

A balm that is compact, convenient to use, and provides instant comfort and protection for those who experience chafing, rashes, and skin irritations due to constant movement. A product that reflects the values of a brand that supports individuals who are always active and on-the-go.

At Motion Ease Co., we envision a world where comfort is accessible to all, enabling active lifestyles through promoting inclusivity, self-confidence, and ease in every step.
